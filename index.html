MERCURY

Mercury is the smallest and closest planet to the Sun in our solar system. It is named after the Roman messenger god, Mercury, due to its fast orbit around the Sun. The planet's surface is covered in craters, and it has extreme temperature variations, with scorching hot days and freezing cold nights.
